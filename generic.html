About
Myself

Hello! My name is Suzan Subedi, and I’m a passionate student and a budding content creator on YouTube. Born on March 26, 2005, and currently living in the vibrant city of Kathmandu, Nepal, I’ve always been driven by curiosity and creativity. As a student, I’m dedicated to learning and expanding my knowledge in every way possible. Balancing academics with my creative pursuits has been both a challenge and a joy. My YouTube channel is a reflection of my diverse interests, where I share engaging content that resonates with people who love entertainment and inspiration. Beyond academics and content creation, I’m an avid movie enthusiast. Watching films is not just a pastime for me but a way to explore storytelling and creativity. Singing is another passion that adds melody to my life, allowing me to express myself and connect with others through music. .

I also enjoy exploring new places and experiencing different cultures. Traveling inspires me and fuels my creativity, giving me fresh perspectives to share with my audience. Additionally, I love diving into books that range from fiction to self-development, as they help me grow and expand my understanding of the world. Through my journey, I aim to inspire and connect with like-minded individuals, sharing pieces of my world while learning from theirs. Thank you for being part of my story!
