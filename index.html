February 14, 2025(USA)

Captain America:
Brave New World

Captain America: Brave New World: This film is the fourth installment in the Captain America film series and is set to be released on February 14, 2025. It is directed by Julius Onah and stars Anthony Mackie as Sam Wilson, who has taken up the mantle of Captain America. The plot involves Sam Wilson finding himself at the center of an international incident after meeting with newly elected U.S. President Thaddeus Ross. He must uncover the motive behind a nefarious global plan. The film is not part of the Avengers series but is a continuation of the storyline from the television miniseries "The Falcon and the Winter Soldier" (2021).

May 7, 2027 (USA)

Avengers: Secret Wars

Avengers: Secret Wars is the sixth Avengers film and is set to be released on May 7, 2027. It is part of Phase Six of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) and will feature several heroes from previous phases battling a cosmic threat that rivals Thanos. The film borrows elements from the Marvel Comics event of the same name. The Russos, along with Christopher Markus and Steven McFeely, are returning to work on the film after the idea of bringing the Secret Wars comic arc to the MCU was proposed. The film is the forty-first in the MCU and is the fourth and final installment of Phase Six and The Multiverse Saga.

October 2, 2025 (India)

Kantara: Chapter 1

Kantara: Chapter 1 is an upcoming Indian Kannada-language period horror fantasy action thriller film set during the reign of Kadambas of Banavasi written and directed by Rishab Shetty, and produced by Vijay Kiragandur, under Hombale Films. It is a prequel to the 2022 film Kantara.
