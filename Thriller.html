Maharaja

Maharaja (2024) is an intense action thriller directed by Nithilan Swaminathan, starring Vijay Sethupathi in his 50th film. The story follows a barber named Maharaja, who reports a burglary to the police, cryptically stating that his "Lakshmi" has been taken. This initially creates confusion, but as the investigation unfolds, the true nature of the theft—and its deeper implications—gradually come to light. The film employs a non-linear narrative, switching between past and present to build suspense. It explores themes of justice, vengeance, and moral dilemmas, with strong performances, especially from Sethupathi and Anurag Kashyap, who plays a chilling antagonist. The cinematography and intense action sequences further enhance the gripping storytelling​ . Maharaja is available for streaming on Netflix.
